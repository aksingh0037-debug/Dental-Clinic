About Lal Dental Clinic

8 Years of Caring for Your Smile

Founded in 2018, Lal Dental Clinic has been a trusted name in oral healthcare in Greater Faridabad, Sec 82. Led by experienced dental professionals, we offer a wide range of treatments including root canal treatment, dental fillings, tooth extraction, scaling & polishing, smile designing, braces consultation, crowns & bridges, dentures, and preventive dental care.

🏥
State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Fully equipped with the latest dental technology including 3D imaging, laser dentistry, and digital X-rays for precise diagnosis.

🧼
Stringent Sterilisation Protocols

We follow international standards for infection control, ensuring your safety with single-use consumables and autoclave sterilisation.
